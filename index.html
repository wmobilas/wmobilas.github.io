University of Students
The University of Students is based on the idea that the student has the inalienable right to the freedom of his education: deciding what to study, how to study, with whom to study, when to study, where to study, why to study, what education means to him

Our mission
Mankind is getting progressively stupider and losing IQ points every year; a high-quality education is still available to a small percentage of the world's population. Meanwhile, modern educational systems and institutions have not changed since the beginning of the industrial age, they are fundamentally outdated and no longer able to answer the challenges of our time. They standardize and turn students into mere cogs in the system rather than fully realized and developed individuals. With your participation, we can give everyone the opportunity to discover their inner potential and to fulfil themselves as a fully developed person.

The University of the Students exists for the students and is carried out by and through the students.
Student-Centered
Education
A Student of the University of the Students is the author of his or her own education. The student can solve emerging issues on his/her own, with other students, and/or with a teacher, and/or with a mentor
Rich Learning
Environment
As we see it, the University of Students itself should include a diverse social, expert, dialogic, cultural and pedagogical environment and support for student self-directed learning
